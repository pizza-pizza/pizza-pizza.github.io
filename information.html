Information

We are a Berlin based studio of strategists, designers and developers that approaches design and development in a holistic and systematic way in order to make a lasting impact.

Holistic: we consider the big picture in everything we do. Systematic: instead of “spray and pray” our design process is very strategic driven.

Projects that excite us the most are the ones that satisfy us intellectually as well as creatively. In approaching them we don’t follow a standardized process. Instead, we put a huge emphasize on the people involved. Plans don’t push things forward – curious, optimistic, determined people do.

To keep productivity high – and politics at bay – the team is never so big that two pizzas couldn’t feed it.

Operating Model

Service provider to clients and agencies

We help with design, marketing and development services. The extent of the service package varies from quick sprints within weeks to more complex stuff that constitutes several successive months of work.

Co-founder for founders

We support entrepreneurs on all activities that influence the direction of the firm: business model design, marketing, branding, development, capital, thinking through issues and make use of our network.

Incubator

We develop ideas on our own and see where they lead us. If the further explored idea looks promising we spin it off into a separate company and continue as a regular startup.
